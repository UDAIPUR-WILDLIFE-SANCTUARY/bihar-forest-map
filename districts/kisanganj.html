Raniganj Vriksha Vatika

Raniganj Vriksha Vatika is the forest land notified by the Bihar government. The ‘Raniganj Vriksh Vatika’ area was formerly known as Hasanpur Balu Dhima. This is spread over a large area of 289 acres has been developed as a natural forest area. Its changed nature has been given attractive look as per the needs of tourists. its floura and fauna is very diverse that has 8 Deers, 32 anaconda and dozens of variety of migrated birds.
