🐬 Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary

Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary, located along the Ganges in Bihar, is India’s only protected area for the endangered Gangetic dolphin. Spanning around 50 km, it shelters rich aquatic biodiversity including turtles, otters, and rare fish species. This sanctuary is vital for dolphin conservation and serves as an important site for research, eco-tourism, and raising awareness about freshwater ecosystems.
