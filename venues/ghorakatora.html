🌊 Ghora Katora Lake

Ghora Katora is a picturesque, horse-shaped lake nestled in the hills near Rajgir, Bihar. Surrounded by lush greenery, it's an eco-friendly, vehicle-free zone ideal for boating, cycling, and nature walks. With breathtaking views and peaceful ambiance, it attracts tourists, families, and nature lovers alike. The site promotes sustainable tourism while offering a refreshing escape from city chaos.
