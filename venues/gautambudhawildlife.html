🌳 Gautam Buddha Wildlife Sanctuary

Located across Bihar's Gaya and Jharkhand's Koderma districts, Gautam Buddha Wildlife Sanctuary was established in 1976 over 259 km². Formerly a private hunting reserve during the British Raj, it now serves as a vital protected habitat. The sanctuary features dry deciduous forests, hills, and diverse wildlife including deer, leopards, and birds, making it a key conservation and eco-tourism site.
