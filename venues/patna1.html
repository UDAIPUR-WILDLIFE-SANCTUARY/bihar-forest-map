🌿 Rajdhani Vatika (Eco Park)

Eco Park, also known as Rajdhani Vatika, is a sprawling green sanctuary situated in Patna, the capital of Bihar, India. Established in October 2011 by the government of Bihar, this park covers an area of over 9.18 hectares and is situated on Strand Road, alongside the southern bank of the river Ganges. It aims to provide a place of relaxation and recreation while promoting eco-conservation, bio-diversity, and awareness of natural resources.
