🕊️ Kusheshwar Asthan Bird Sanctuary

Established in 1994, Kusheshwar Asthan Bird Sanctuary in Darbhanga, Bihar, encompasses 14 waterlogged villages over 7,019 acres. It serves as a vital winter refuge for over 160 bird species, including endangered migratory birds like the Siberian crane and Dalmatian pelican. The sanctuary's rich biodiversity and serene wetlands attract birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts from across the region.
