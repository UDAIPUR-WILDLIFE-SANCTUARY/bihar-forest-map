💧 Gokul Jalashya

Gokul Jalashya, located in Buxar district, is a vital ecological asset. It serves as a habitat for diverse aquatic species and seasonal migratory birds, supporting local biodiversity. The wetland aids in groundwater recharge and agricultural irrigation, making it crucial for sustainable farming. It also provides livelihoods through fishing and related activities, reinforcing its environmental and socio-economic significance.
