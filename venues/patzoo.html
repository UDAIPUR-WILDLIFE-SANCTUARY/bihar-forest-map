🌿 Sanjay Gandhi Biological Park (Patna Zoo)

Sanjay Gandhi Biological Park—aka Patna Zoo—is the ultimate chill spot in the city. This 153-acre green haven started as a botanical garden in 1969 and leveled up to a full-fledged zoo by 1973. Now, it's a vibe with over 800 animals from 110+ species, including Bengal and white tigers, giraffes, rhinos, and chimps.
