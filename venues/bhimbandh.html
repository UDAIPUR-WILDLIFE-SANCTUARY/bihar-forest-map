🦁 Bhimbandh Wildlife Sanctuary

Bhimbandh Wildlife Sanctuary in Munger, Bihar, is a hidden ecological gem famous for its hot springs, lush forests, and diverse wildlife. Spanning hills and valleys, it shelters tigers, leopards, deer, and numerous bird species. The sanctuary's untouched natural beauty and thermal springs make it ideal for nature lovers, researchers, and eco-tourists seeking serenity and adventure in the wild.
