Kanwar Lake Sanctuary

Kanwar Lake Bird Sanctuary, located in Begusarai, Bihar, encompasses Asia's largest freshwater oxbow lake, formed by the meandering Burhi Gandak River. This Ramsar-listed wetland supports over 220 bird species, including migratory waterbirds along the Central Asian Flyway. The sanctuary plays a vital role in regional biodiversity, flood control, and groundwater recharge, making it essential for ecological balance.
