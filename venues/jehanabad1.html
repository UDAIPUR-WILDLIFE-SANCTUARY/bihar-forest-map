Gaya

Gaya’s eco-tourism combines spiritual heritage with nature and wildlife exploration. Sites like Gautam Buddha Wildlife Sanctuary promote conservation and environmental awareness. With rich forests, scenic hills, and diverse fauna, Gaya offers a peaceful yet educational escape.

| Gautam Buddha Wildlife Sanctuary |



Gautam Buddha Wildlife Sanctuary, spanning Gaya (Bihar), was established in 1976. Once a British-era hunting reserve, it now protects 259 km² of rich biodiversity and forest.
